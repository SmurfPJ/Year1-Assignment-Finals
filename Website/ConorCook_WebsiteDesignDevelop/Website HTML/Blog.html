Foothills walking festival postponed

The Methven & Foothills walking festival has been postponed to April 2023 but that doesn't mean you cant still come visit and participate - from the Saturday 16th to the 24th April they have a few smaller events running alongside our local Scarecrow competition. So if your looking to do something new this Autumn then come stay and check it out.
